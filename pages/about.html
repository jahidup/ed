Who We Are

Shaping Future Leaders Since 2009

Future Edge Academy was founded in 2009 with a singular vision: to create an educational ecosystem where technology, creativity, and human potential converge. Over 15 years, we have grown from a small campus of 200 students to a thriving community of over 5,000 learners.

We are affiliated with CBSE and recognized as one of India's top-10 technology-integrated schools. Our campus features world-class infrastructure, including AI labs, robotics workshops, an Olympic-grade sports complex, and a digital library with over 50,000 resources.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

2009
The Beginning

Future Edge Academy was founded by visionary educator Dr. Rajiv Kumar with 200 students, 15 teachers, and a dream to redefine Indian education.

2012
CBSE Affiliation & Expansion

Received CBSE affiliation and expanded to secondary classes. Introduced the first computer lab with 80 systems and high-speed internet.

2015
Smart Classroom Revolution

Installed 60+ smart classrooms with interactive whiteboards and launched our first robotics club, winning 3 national competitions in the first year.

2018
Senior Secondary Launch

Added Classes XI–XII with Science, Commerce, and Arts streams. First batch produced 12 IIT and 8 NIT selections.

2021
AI & Innovation Center

Inaugurated India's first school-level AI Research & Innovation Center, partnering with IIT Delhi and Microsoft Education.

2024
Ranked #1 Futuristic School

Awarded India's #1 Technology-Integrated School by the National Education Excellence Awards. 5,000+ students, 200+ faculty, 350+ trophies.
