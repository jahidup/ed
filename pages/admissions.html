How to Apply

Simple Admission Process

Joining Future Edge Academy is simple and transparent. Follow these steps to secure your child's seat at one of India's most innovative schools.

01
Fill the Online Application

Complete the online application form with student details, preferred class, and parent information. Takes less than 5 minutes.

02
Document Verification

Upload required documents: birth certificate, previous school reports, proof of residence, and passport-size photos.

03
Interaction Session

Attend a brief interaction/assessment session at the campus. It's a friendly conversation, not a formal exam!

04
Seat Confirmation

Receive your selection letter and confirm the seat by submitting the admission fee. Welcome to the Future Edge family! 🎉

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 5–6 years for Class I (age-appropriate for other classes)
  • Previous school report card (for Class II and above)
  • Aadhar card and birth certificate (mandatory)
  • Transfer certificate from previous school (for lateral admissions)
