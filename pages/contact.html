FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school timings?
School hours are Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM for all classes. Extra-curricular and coaching sessions may extend until 5:30 PM.
Is there a bus/transport facility?
Yes! We operate 20+ GPS-tracked school buses covering all major areas of the city. Transport is optional and billed separately. Contact us for route details.
Are scholarships available?
Yes. We offer merit scholarships (up to 100% fee waiver) for top academic performers and need-based scholarships for economically weaker families. Apply during admissions.
Can we schedule a campus visit?
Absolutely! Campus tours are available on weekdays from 10 AM to 1 PM. Please fill out the contact form or call us to schedule your visit.
